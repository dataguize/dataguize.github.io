What does this score means:
The business risk factor category reflects how well your idea is developed or thought through. This category measures the overall health of the idea relative to specific factors such as investment, costs, working model and basic market analysis. Scoring low in this category does not mean that the idea would not be a success. However, it shows that you need to do more research and development work. On the other hand a low score indicate that the idea or innovation needs more detail and it cannot be evaluated properly.

Scoring low in this category could be miss leading since most of these factors gets better defined in latter phase of the idea or innovation evaluation process. However, understanding these factors and the ability to address them helps in better defining the idea or innovation overall market potential. This score coupled with the business risk factors should provide a better understanding of the idea or innovation investment risks.
